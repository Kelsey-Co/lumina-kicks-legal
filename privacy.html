Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Lumina Kicks ("the App") is a fetal movement tracking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data Collection

Lumina Kicks does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. All data — including session records and patient information entered for PDF reports — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SQLite).

3. No Server Communication

The App makes zero network requests. Your data never leaves your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any third-party services.

4. Data Storage

All fetal movement data is stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data at all times. You can delete all data from within the App via Settings → Delete All Data.

5. Data Deletion

If you delete the App or use the "Delete All Data" function, your data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered by us or any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Lumina Kicks is compliant with GDPR and CCPA principles by design — we process zero personal data off-device. Since no data is collected, there is no data to access, correct, or delete on our end.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the App and on this page with an updated effective date.
