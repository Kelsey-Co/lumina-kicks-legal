Medical Disclaimer

⚠️ Important: Lumina Kicks is designed for educational and informational tracking purposes only. It is NOT intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Not a Medical Device

Lumina Kicks is not a medical device and has not been evaluated or approved by the FDA, CE, or any other regulatory body. It is a self-monitoring tool that helps you track fetal movements based on the ACOG Count-to-Ten method.

Consult Your Healthcare Provider

Always seek the advice of your physician, OB-GYN, or other qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or your pregnancy.

Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read or tracked in this App.

Emergency Situations

If you notice a significant decrease in fetal movement or have any concerns about your pregnancy, contact your healthcare provider immediately. Do not rely solely on this App to make medical decisions.

Data Accuracy

The accuracy of the data tracked depends entirely on user input. The App does not independently monitor fetal activity. Reports generated by the App are based on self-reported data and should be reviewed by a qualified medical professional.

ACOG Reference

This App references guidelines from the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG). These guidelines are for general informational purposes. Your healthcare provider may have specific recommendations tailored to your individual pregnancy.
